Who We Are

A licensed, certifiedSection-8 non-profit organization.

Founded on September 23, 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla (Founder & CEO), InAmigos Foundation is licensed by the Central Government of India. Holding 80G, 12A, NITI Aayog, CSR-1, and IAF ISO 9001:2015 certifications, we translate corporate and personal contributions into highly efficient grassroots action.

Our Vision

We envision a highly inclusive, compassionate, and empowered Indian society where absolute transparency governs charity, allowing underprivileged families access to development without barriers.

🎯

Our Mission

To build robust, localized delivery modules for child education, female vocational training, community nutrition drives, animal welfare, and eco-conservation, scaling our impact across 28 Indian states.

🤝

Our Approach

We utilize ISO-certified operating standards, NITI Aayog guidelines, and corporate CSR partnerships to execute fully audited field campaigns, publishing constant updates directly on social media.
